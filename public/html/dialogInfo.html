Further Information

This isn't a real simulation?

No, it isn't. Check out this awesome NASA simulation of our solar system.

What are the assumptions?

This simulation was just made to look pretty and is not scientific. Therefore, almost all parameters, such as the rotation speed, orbit speed, orbit eccentricity or the distance between planets are not correct. All planets have a circular orbit.

What is accurate?

The rotation angles and the corrected size are sort of accurate, although the flucuations of these parametere are not taken into account.

What is the "Corrected ratio" checkbox?

In this state, the planets are resized relative to each other with their actual relative size differences.
