Further Information

Is there a real simulation?

Yes, There is! Check out this awesome simulation by NASA.

What is not accurate?

First off, almost all parameters are adjusted. This simulaltion is just made to look pretty. The orbit tilt, rotation speed, orbit speed and relative distance to the sun are not correct. Furthermore, The orbits are circular and not elliptical.

What is accurate?

The rotation angles and the corrected size are sort of accurate.

What does size corrected mean?

It just gives you an idea of the huge difference in size of the different planets and the sun.
